Lemon Whiskey Slush

A refreshing drink on a hot summer day. An adult version of a lemonade slush.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Irish whiskey
  • 3 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 4 cups of ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place all of the ingrdients into a blender.
  2. Blend and pour into a glass.
  3. Enjoy!
