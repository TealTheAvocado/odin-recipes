Raspberry-Infused Vodka

This recipe is for a raspberry-infused vodka. It is very simple but delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 gallons of vodka
  • 12 ounces of frozen raspberries
  • 4 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. Combine vodka, raspberries, sugar, and water in a large, non-metallic container.
  2. Press and muddle raspberries until incorporated into the mixture.
  3. Allow mixture to sit, stirring for 10 to 15 minutes each day, for 5 to 6 days.
  4. Strain mixture multiple times through a fine-mesh strainer or cheesecloth until berries and seeds are removed and liquid is lighter in color.
  5. Enjoy!
