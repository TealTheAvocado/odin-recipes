Tequila Sunrise

This recipe makes a delicious citrus cocktail. You only need a few ingredients but it's lit.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of freshley squeezed orange juice
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces of jigger tequila
  • 1 1/2 fuild onces of jigger grenadine syrup
  • ice cubes
  • 1 slice of orange for garnish
  • 1 maraschino cherry for garnish

Steps

  1. Stir orange juice and tequila together. Fill a chilled 12-ounce glass with ice cubes; pour in orange juice mixture.
  2. Slowly pour in the grenadine, and allow it to settle to the bottom of the glass (be patient).
  3. Garnish with a slice of orange and a maraschino cherry.
  4. Enjoy!
